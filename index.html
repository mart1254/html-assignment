About Me

Hello! I'm Dominic Martin.
I was born on September3rd, 2000 and I am originally from Aylmer, Gatineau, QC. I moved to Ontario around 2011 and have since stayed in Ottawa and currently live in Nepean on the same street as the IKEA.

I enrolled in Interactive Media Design because I had originally took the Journalism course here at Algonquin and really enjoyed the technical aspects of the program but not so much the actual Journalism part, and was recommended IMD by the program coordinator, but wanted to try my hand at Computer Programming first because at the time I didn't really consider myself a design person. I enrolled into Computer Programming but failed Math twice -not a a math person- and finally enrolled in IMD and I'm loving it!
